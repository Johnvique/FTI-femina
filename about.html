About Femina Institute

School Profile

Femina Training Institute is a school in Meru County,Tigania West sub-county,Kianjai Market that aims at imparting knowledge,information,positive attitude and skills to facilitate faster developement.To be able to achieve this effecetively,the college has established collaborative network with Karma Training Institute of Isiolo that is a TVETA registered institution.

The school has three study modes.We offer full time learning with classes resuming from 8am-4pm,part time classes which are arranged at students' convenience and finally the online distance learning where we send notes to students via whatsapp and receive feedback accordingly.Under this arrangement,we insist on contact hours whereby students have to come to school for atleast 6weeks yearly.

Femina Training Institute prepares the students for the internal examinations as well as booking for them external examinations of Kenya National Examination Council(KNEC),Kenya Accountants and Secretaries National Examination Board and Computer Society Of Kenya.The college has highly qualified teachers who are very passionate on their areas of specialization.We do carry out three intakes every year especially in January,May and September.
